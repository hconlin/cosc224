This site is administered and maintained by the Okanagan College Computer Science Department

Mission Statement

The Okanagan College COSC Event Club provides students with information on a variety of events while providing event coordinators an avenue to spread event details.

How This site works

Students may sign up to receive information on a variety of different events based on their preferences.

After confirming their email, the registered users will receive emails pertaining to the event types they have chosen to hear about.

Events of all flavors

This site will keep track of a variety of COSC related events including the following:

  • Game Competitions
  • Software Competitions
  • Meet & Greets
  • Seminars
  • Social Networking
  • Jobs Fairs
